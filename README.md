# Doom remake
Nothing worth while. 

Controls:<br>
`W` to go forward<br>
`A` to strafe left<br>
`S` to go backward<br>
`D` to strafe right<br>
`M+W` to move up<br>
`M+A` to look down<br>
`M+S` to move down<br>
`M+D` to look up<br>
`<` to look left<br>
`>` to look right<br>
